Account & Data Deletion

How to delete your ImportantFirst account and associated data

Delete your account in the app (recommended)

You can delete your account directly from the ImportantFirst mobile app:

  1. Open ImportantFirst
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Open Danger Zone
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm

What happens when you delete your account

When you request account deletion:

  • Your account will be deactivated
  • Your tasks and associated account data will be scheduled for permanent deletion
  • Deletion is intended to be irreversible

If you cannot access the app

If you can’t sign in (for example, you lost access to your device), you can request deletion by email:

Email: privacy@importantfirst.app

Please include:

  • The email address of the account to be deleted
  • Your display name (if you set one)
  • The words “Account deletion request” in the subject

For help with account access issues, see our Support page.
